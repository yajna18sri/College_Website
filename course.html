Field Work

Fieldwork is hands-on experience gained outside the classroom, where individuals apply theoretical knowledge to real-world situations. In fields like veterinary dairying, it involves tasks such as animal care and farm management, helping to build practical skills and enhance learning.

Dairying

Dairying involves the care and management of dairy animals, primarily cows, for the production of milk and other dairy products. It includes activities such as milking, feeding, breeding, and maintaining the health of the animals, with the goal of producing high-quality dairy products for consumption.

Veterinary

Veterinary science focuses on the health and care of animals. It involves diagnosing and treating diseases, injuries, and illnesses in animals, as well as providing preventative care such as vaccinations. Veterinarians play a crucial role in ensuring the well-being of pets, livestock, and wildlife.

Nursing

Nursing involves providing care and support to patients, focusing on their physical, emotional, and mental well-being. Nurses assess patients' conditions, administer treatments, monitor progress, and offer guidance to help patients recover. They play a vital role in the healthcare team, ensuring that patients receive the necessary care and attention throughout their recovery process.
